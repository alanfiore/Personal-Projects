Meet the Band




Bio for "The Marathon" featuring Gavin:

Gavin is the talented bass player for the band, "The Marathon". With over a decade of experience in the music industry, Gavin brings a unique blend of rhythm and melody to the group's sound. His deep bass lines create a solid foundation for the band's music, while his creative approach to songwriting adds a layer of complexity and depth to their performances. When he's not on stage with the band, Gavin enjoys exploring new musical genres and experimenting with different techniques to push the boundaries of his craft. Gavin's passion for music is only matched by his love of running. He enjoys long distance runs, which he says help him clear his mind and stay focused during band rehearsals and performances.

Bio for "The Marathon" featuring Quinn:

Quinn is the energetic drummer for "The Marathon". With a background in jazz and a passion for funk, Quinn's rhythmic style is both dynamic and expressive. His hard-hitting beats and intricate fills drive the band's sound, providing a powerful backbone for their performances. When he's not behind the kit, Quinn can be found practicing new techniques and exploring new rhythms to bring to the band's music. Quinn's rhythmic style on the drums is a reflection of his love of running. He often listens to high-energy music while on his runs, which helps him stay in shape and build his stamina for long sets with the band.

Bio for "The Marathon" featuring Alan:

Alan is the versatile guitarist for "The Marathon". With a degree in music theory and composition, Alan brings a unique blend of technical skill and creative flair to the band's sound. His melodic guitar solos and intricate riffs add a layer of complexity to their music, while his intuitive sense of rhythm keeps the band's performances tight and cohesive. When he's not on stage with the band, Alan can be found writing and recording his own music, experimenting with new sounds and textures. Alan's technical skill on the guitar is a result of his discipline and dedication to running. He sees a strong correlation between the two activities, as both require consistent practice and a commitment to pushing oneself to the limit.

Bio for "The Marathon" featuring Vicente:

Vicente is the talented tenor sax player for "The Marathon". With a background in jazz and a passion for soul, Vicente's playing is both soulful and expressive. His melodic sax lines add a layer of depth and emotion to the band's sound, creating a unique and memorable experience for audiences. When he's not on stage with the band, Vicente enjoys exploring new musical genres and collaborating with other musicians to push the boundaries of his craft. Vicente's soulful sax playing is inspired by his love of running. He often finds that the endorphins released during his runs help him tap into a deeper emotional space when performing with the band.
